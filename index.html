Avicii - Tim Bergling

This is The Timeline of Avicii's Life:

  • 1989 - Born in Stockholm, Sweden
  • 1997 - Started mixing at the age of eight
  • 2005 - Started making Music at the age of 16
  • 2007 - Signed on with his very first label Dejfitts Plays
  • 2010 - Released his first hit song Seek Bromance
    - Begun his international live shows at the Guvernment, Toronto, Canada
  • 2013 - Won the 2013 Grammy Award for levels in the EDM category
    - His song Wake Me Up became number one on Spotify global chart
    - Released his debut studio album True
  • 2015 - Released his second album Stories
  • 2016 - Last show at Ushuaïa Ibiza Beach Hotel, Spain
  • 2017 - Released his third album titled Avīci (01)
  • 2018 - Died at age 28 in Muscat, Oman
